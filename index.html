Shuangjia (Edgar) Zheng

I'm a Ph.D student in Computer Science and Technology at the Sun Yat-Sen University, supervised by Yuedong Yang.

My research interests lie in the deep learning, text mining, drug discovery and computational biology. I am dedicated to bringing deep learning methods to drug development.

Previously, I completed my MSc in Medicinal Chemistry and Cheminformatics with Jun Xu and had been undergrad research assistant for ZhiQiang Yu.
